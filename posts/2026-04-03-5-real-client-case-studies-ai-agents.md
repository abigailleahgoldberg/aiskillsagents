# 5 Real Client Case Studies: What Happened When We Deployed AI Agents

Theory is nice. Results are better. Here are five real deployments — what we built, what happened, and what we learned.

## Case Study 1: Regional HVAC Company — Lead Response Agent

**The Problem:** A 12-person HVAC company was getting 40-60 leads per week but couldn't staff evening and weekend response. By Monday morning, a significant percentage had already booked a competitor.

**What We Built:** An AI agent on their website and CRM that acknowledged leads immediately, asked qualifying questions, checked service area, scheduled callbacks for qualified leads, and sent priority-flagged leads to the team for after-hours inquiries.

**Results (90 days):**
- Lead response time: 4.2 hours → 1.8 minutes
- Leads lost to no-response: 34% → 8%
- Close rate on inbound leads: 22% → 31%
- Additional monthly revenue: $18,000-24,000

**Lesson learned:** The agent initially asked for a phone number too quickly. Letting the conversation develop for 2-3 exchanges before requesting contact improved close rate by 9%.

## Case Study 2: Solo Estate Planning Attorney — Pre-Consultation Agent

**The Problem:** 6 hours/week spent on initial consultations that didn't convert.

**What We Built:** A pre-consultation AI resource that walked prospects through estate planning basics, asked a structured intake questionnaire, generated personalized summaries of recommendations, and offered to schedule paid consultations with intake data pre-prepared.

**Results (60 days):**
- Hours/week on non-converting calls: 6 → 1.5
- Conversion rate of booked consultations to retained clients: 41% → 68%
- Average new clients per month: +2.3

## Case Study 3: E-Commerce Brand — Customer Service Agent

**The Problem:** 80% of customer service tickets were the same 12 questions. Staff spent 70% of their time on repetitive queries.

**What We Built:** A customer service AI trained on all product documentation, policy, and previous tickets with real-time order lookup integration and clear escalation criteria.

**Results (45 days):**
- Tickets resolved without human involvement: 74%
- Average response time: 6.2 hours → 8 minutes
- Customer satisfaction score: 3.8/5 → 4.4/5
- Staff hours redirected to strategic work: +21/week

## Case Study 4: Staffing Agency — Candidate Screening Agent

**The Problem:** Recruiters spending 60-70% of their time on initial resume review and screening calls.

**What We Built:** An AI screening agent integrated with their ATS that analyzed applications, sent structured questionnaires to qualified candidates, scored and ranked responses, and flagged top candidates with summary briefs.

**Results (90 days):**
- Recruiter time on initial screening: 68% → 24% of workweek
- Time-to-first-interview: 8.3 days → 2.1 days
- Placements per recruiter per month: +1.4 average

## Case Study 5: B2B SaaS — Trial Onboarding Agent

**The Problem:** 67% of 14-day trial users never completed their first meaningful action in the product.

**What We Built:** An in-product onboarding agent that personalized flows based on use case, proactively messaged inactive users after 24 hours, answered questions in-context, and flagged high-intent users for sales outreach.

**Results (60 days):**
- Trials completing first meaningful action: 33% → 61%
- Trial-to-paid conversion: 18% → 27%
- Time-to-activation: 4.2 days → 1.8 days

**The biggest win:** The 24-hour inactivity trigger. Most trial churn is "got busy and forgot" — not "tried it, didn't like it."

---

**What these five cases have in common:** The problem was specific and measurable. The agent augmented humans rather than replacing them. Iteration mattered more than perfection at launch. ROI was faster than expected — fastest payback was 23 days, slowest was 91.

The technology is ready. The question is whether your business is ready to use it well.
